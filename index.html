About Area 24

Welcome to AREA 24 - The Interior Hub, your trusted partner in creating spaces that blend aesthetics with functionality. Founded in 2022 by Mr. Dhiraj Pahelani & Mr. Parth Baldha, we stand as a testament to excellence in interior management services. Based in Ahmedabad, our team of build specialists is dedicated to delivering top-notch, methodical, technological, and sustainable fit-out solutions.

Our guiding motto is to maximize utility and economic returns for our clients. We achieve this by providing specialized skills tailored to your unique project requirements. Whether you're envisioning a modern office space, a cozy home interior, or a commercial establishment with a distinct personality, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.
