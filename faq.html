Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

General Questions

What services does Area 24 offer?

Area 24 specializes in comprehensive interior design and architectural solutions. Our services include residential and commercial interior design, 3D visualization, 2D layout planning, turnkey project execution, Vastu consultancy, and complete project management from concept to completion.

Do you work with both residential and commercial projects?

Yes, we handle both residential and commercial projects. Our expertise spans homes, apartments, offices, retail spaces, restaurants, hotels, and various other commercial establishments. Each project type receives specialized attention based on its unique requirements.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve clients in Gandhinagar and surrounding areas in Gujarat. However, we also work with clients across India for larger commercial projects and can accommodate outstation clients based on project scope and requirements.

Design Process

How long does a typical interior design project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A residential interior design project typically takes 4-8 weeks for the design phase, while commercial projects may take 6-12 weeks. Turnkey projects including construction can range from 3-12 months depending on the scale.

What is the first step in working with Area 24?

The process begins with an initial consultation where we discuss your vision, requirements, budget, and timeline. We'll visit your space (if applicable), understand your lifestyle needs, and provide preliminary design concepts to ensure we're aligned before proceeding.

Do you provide 3D visualizations?

Absolutely! We use advanced 3D visualization software to create realistic renderings of your space. This helps you visualize the final outcome before any work begins, allowing for modifications and ensuring complete satisfaction with the design.

Can I make changes during the design process?

Yes, we encourage client feedback throughout the process. We typically provide 2-3 rounds of revisions for each design phase. Our collaborative approach ensures the final design perfectly matches your vision and requirements.

Pricing & Budget

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on project scope, complexity, and services required. We offer flexible packages starting from basic consultation to comprehensive turnkey solutions. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed quote based on your specific needs and budget.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we understand that design and construction projects involve significant investment. We offer structured payment plans that align with project milestones, making it easier to manage cash flow throughout the project duration.

What is included in your turnkey solutions?

Our turnkey solutions include everything from initial design concept through to final execution. This encompasses design, procurement of materials, construction, installation, and project management. We coordinate all aspects to deliver a seamless experience.

Materials & Quality

What quality standards do you maintain?

We maintain the highest quality standards across all our projects. We work with certified vendors, use premium materials, and follow industry best practices. All our work comes with warranties and we ensure compliance with local building codes and safety standards.

Do you use sustainable materials?

Yes, sustainability is important to us. We offer eco-friendly material options including recycled materials, low-VOC paints, sustainable wood alternatives, and energy-efficient solutions. We can help you achieve green building certifications if desired.

Can I specify particular materials or brands?

Absolutely! If you have preferred materials, brands, or suppliers, we'll incorporate them into your project. We can also recommend options based on quality, durability, aesthetics, and budget considerations.

We're here to help! Contact us for personalized answers to your specific needs.
