About Area 24

Welcome to AREA 24 - The Interior Creators, your trusted partner in creating spaces that blend aesthetics with functionality. Founded by Mr. Dhiraj Pahelani and Mr. Parth Pahelani, we stand as a testament to excellence in interior management services. Based in Gandhinagar, our team of build specialists is dedicated to delivering top-notch, methodical, and sustainable fit-out solutions.

Our guiding principle is simple: maximize utility and economic returns for every client through specialized skills tailored to your unique project requirements. Whether you're envisioning a modern office space, a cozy home interior, or a commercial establishment with a distinct personality, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.
